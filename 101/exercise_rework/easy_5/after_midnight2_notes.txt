# after_midnight2_notes.txt

Understanding:
  - Two Methods
  - Inputs
    > String
    > Represents a time of the day
  - Task
    > Determine minutes before or after midnight
    > '00:00' is midnight
    > '24:00' is midnight
  - Outputs
    > Integers

Approach:
  - Split the string by ':'
  - Modulus hours by 24 to get the hours
  - Add minutes to the hours
  - Add or subtract from the total minutes in a day
